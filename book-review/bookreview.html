1.Don't Believe Everything You Think (English)

Don't Believe Everything You Think" is the first book I read of Joseph Nguyen. A fine comprehension and compilation of thoughts and thinking! A beautiful perspective leading to a happy living! Guides and evolves us to get out of the loop of unwanted thinking and throws light on how one can live life happily which is the most natural and ideal way of living! 2. YOU ONLY LIVE ONCE

"You Only Live Once" delves into the enigmatic disappearance of renowned singer Elisha, intertwining the lives of a broken YouTube star, a hopeful comedian, and a laid-back beach shack owner in the search. As they unravel Elisha's mystery, they navigate love, identity, and self-discovery. An intriguing tale of lost connections and found truths.

3. THE SILENT PATIENT

I bought this book after much persuasion by my friend, although there are some obscenities in the book, but the book was gripping and kept me addicted to the last page, and God! The twist in the last chapter left me speechless. The in-depth writing, expressing the emotions of characters, is very rare to find in books of mystery genre since they mainly focus on the plot, but this is where the book is extraordinary, loved reading the emotional turmoil and the writer's own theses about the human-psych. Highly recommended for crime enthusiasts!

6. Kunex It Ends With Us : ''Sometimes The One Who Loves You Is The One Who Hurts You The Most''

Beautifully carved out tale. People can relate to the story because it does happen and you do find yourself at crossroads. Sometimes you give in sometimes you don't but the guilt when you don't is very hard to not believe in. Hope is what people feed on throughout the journey and that is exactly what this story offers. Hope, courage and love for humans and their humane emotions. Must read!!

7. A Man Called Ove

This is an amazing book that perfectly captures life in all its highs and lows. It made me laugh out loud at times and cry at others. At no point does it leave you untouched. Be ware to grab a box of tissues as you near the end. Left me sobbing but so life-affirming. Don’t hesitate to read this
